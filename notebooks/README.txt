===================
notebooks
===================

This folder contains jupyter notebooks where the monitoring method is applied to the long-term errors of the composite 'Nc'. 
Files with '365' in their extension indicate that the method is applied to the data smoothed on 365 days. Whereas files with '27' in their extension indicate that the method is applied to the data smoothed on 27 days. 
The notebooks may be viewed as examples of the application of the package to real datasets. 

The notebook 'errors' details the procedure to extract the long-term error. It also explains how to compute the short-term error, the error at minima, the reference of the network and the other desired products that are not directly related to the monitoring.
